FAQ //

Q: I am interested to get in touch! How may I do so?
A: You may send your enquiries on Whatsapp and I will get back to you in 48 hours.

Q: Are the artworks original?
A: All the artworks are painted by me. Each piece takes 3 to 15 days to plan, sketch, and paint, before it is complete.

Q: How do I pay for the paintings?
A: Bank transfers can be arranged for both Thai residents and International friends.

Q: How does delivery work? When can I receive it?
A: Delivery can be arranged with local or international couriers. Delivery fees and schedule will depend on the receipient's shipping address.

Q: Do you do commissioned work?
A: Yes! Happy to hear what you have in mind. Please feel free to contact me for a disussion.
